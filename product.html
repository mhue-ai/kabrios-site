The operating flow

Six steps to continuous readiness.

Step 01

Capture the right evidence

Connect your cloud tools and let Kabrios automatically collect configuration data, posture findings, and policy artifacts. Evidence arrives with context, ownership, and review history — not as orphaned screenshots in a shared drive.

  • Automated evidence from AWS, Azure, GCP
  • Context and history preserved automatically
  • No more screenshot hunting before audits

Screenshot: Evidence collection dashboard

Screenshot: Framework mapping view

Step 02

Map controls to frameworks

Kabrios maps your security controls to the frameworks your business carries — SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST 800-53, CMMC, FedRAMP, GovRamp, and more. AI identifies gaps and suggests what to prioritize first.

  • Pre-built mappings for major frameworks
  • AI identifies coverage gaps automatically
  • Cross-framework control reuse
Step 03

Monitor and review signals

Continuous monitoring surfaces important changes before they create unseen exposure. Kabrios reviews posture drift, configuration changes, and threat detections — then tells your team exactly what needs attention.

  • Real-time posture and drift detection
  • AI-prioritized alerts — not alert fatigue
  • Daily ranked actions, not buried notifications

Screenshot: Monitoring dashboard with AI priorities

Screenshot: Remediation workflow with ownership

Step 04

Remediate with accountability

Work gets assigned, followed up, and closed with proof. AI prioritizes by risk, suggests remediation approaches, and keeps owners accountable with reminders and escalation — so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • AI-prioritized remediation queue
  • Clear ownership and deadlines
  • Automatic follow-up and escalation
Step 05

Report to the right audience

Translate day-to-day operational work into reporting for the audience that needs it — managers, executives, clients, auditors, and board-level stakeholders. Each gets the view they care about, generated from live data.

  • Audience-aware report generation
  • Board-ready executive summaries
  • Auditor collaboration views

Screenshot: Executive reporting dashboard

Screenshot: Continuous readiness overview

Step 06

Repeat the cadence

Readiness is a maintained state, not a one-time project. Kabrios keeps the operating rhythm going — so compliance and trust don't depend on the next audit panic or renewal scramble.

  • Continuous operating cadence
  • AI maintains momentum between audits
  • Year-round readiness, not annual scrambles
