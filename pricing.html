FAQ

Clear answers, up front

Does Kabrios offer a free tier?

Yes. Starter is a $0 tier with 1 framework, 2 data integrations, and 2 complete workflows so you can prove the value of the Kabrios operating model before paying.

How does annual pricing work?

A 1-year commitment costs 10 months of the monthly price. Professional is $199/month or $1,990/year base, and each additional framework is $49/month or $490/year.

How does Professional pricing scale?

Professional includes all workflows and 2 frameworks. If you need more framework coverage, each additional framework adds $49/month using the same annual discount structure if you commit for a year.

Do I need to replace my existing tools?

No. Kabrios is designed to fit around the tools you already use and turn them into a more usable operating rhythm.
