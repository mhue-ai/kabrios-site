How it works

Three steps to continuous readiness.

Connect your tools

Plug in your existing AWS, Azure, or GCP security services. Kabrios ingests findings, posture signals, evidence, and configuration data from the tools you already trust.

AI maps and prioritizes

Kabrios maps your data to frameworks (SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST 800-53, CMMC, FedRAMP), identifies gaps, and builds a prioritized action plan with clear owners.

Your team executes with guidance

AI-driven next steps, reminders, and accountability keep the work moving. Evidence collects automatically. Reports generate for auditors, leadership, and clients.
