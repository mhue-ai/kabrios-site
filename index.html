About us

Built by practitioners. Designed for results.

Kabrios was built by leaders who spent decades dealing with fragmented tooling, manual evidence collection, and point-in-time audit preparation. AI finally made it possible to build the platform we always wanted to use.

Built by security and compliance leaders with 25+ years of real-world experience

Kabrios was founded by operators with more than 25 years of combined experience across cybersecurity, compliance, risk, and audit readiness. We have seen firsthand how too many teams are forced to stitch together spreadsheets, screenshots, ticketing systems, point tools, and consulting-heavy processes just to answer basic readiness questions.

For years, the platform we wanted did not exist at a price point that made sense to build or bring to market. With the rise of AI, automation, and modern cloud architecture, that changed.

Kabrios is the result: a platform designed to unify evidence, monitoring, workflows, and leadership visibility in one system—so teams can spend less time chasing artifacts and more time improving their actual security and compliance posture.

A Results as a Service approach for continuous readiness

Kabrios takes a Results as a Service approach: we focus on helping customers achieve measurable readiness outcomes, not just giving them another dashboard to manage.

Customers select the control framework that applies to their business—such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, NIST-based programs, and other evolving requirements—and Kabrios helps organize the work around that reality.
