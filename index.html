FAQ

Questions teams usually ask

Is Kabrios just for cybersecurity teams?

No. Kabrios is designed for security, compliance, GRC, engineering, internal audit, and leadership teams that need a shared view of readiness.

Can Kabrios support multiple frameworks at once?

Yes. The platform is positioned around control mapping and reusable evidence so one operational signal can support multiple framework views.

Does continuous monitoring replace audits?

No. It makes audits easier by keeping evidence current, exposing drift early, and reducing last-minute collection work.
